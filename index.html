A megalomaniac has successfully researched and developed nuclear warheads, he plans to rain down fire and fury on the major cities of the world. It's your job to stop him!

  • Click on the start button to enter the game screen, then push the red button to begin the defence of planet Earth.
  • Click on the cities targeted by nuclear warheads to enable their defence mechanisms.
  • You can also use keys: Q, W, E and R on your keyboard to save the corresponding cities.
  • You gain points by successfully deflecting missiles. However, if you fail to do this then watch the death toll skyrocket!
  • When your death toll becomes too high, the game ends...
